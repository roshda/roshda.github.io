I don't write about this much, but I've been undergoing treatment for a form of lymphoma for the past couple years. I stand in front of a panel of UVB light for a few minutes, and when the bulbs turn off, I record the remaining lesions into a spreadsheet (a tool I propsed creating myself, which has already helped in identifying better treatment plans).

A while ago I had the idea to get more comprehensive insights, and also automate the tedious counting process using computer vision. Since it's summer now and I have time, here's how I built [the Lymphoma Lens](https://github.com/roshda/lymphoma-lens).

---
title: Origami tips
publishedAt: 2023-10-12
---

- Practice folding what you like
- start small and simple
    - if you don't, you'll fail trying to fold the hard models fast and become discouraged
        - I think this is good general life advice actually
- follow inspiring artists on instagram
    - there's also a big global community on twitter
- paper type only matters for super complex origami


As with most things, the only way to get better is to actually do it.

OrigamiUSA Convention

07-20-2023

The OrigamUSA convention hosted in NYC this weekend was awesome!

I met some of the coolest minds in origami and took 10 classes over 3 days.

Some highlights were:

  • meeting my favorite artists: Boice Wong, John Montroll, Brian Chan Meenakshi Mukherji, and Robert Lang
  • learning origami from the special guets, Miyuki Kawamura and Bodo Haag
  • witnessing the Guinness World Record "largest origami swan" being folded
  • meeting other awesome folders from across the country
  • seeing an exhibit of some of the best orrigami in the world, and displaying some of my own works at the exhibit
  • grabbing lots of new paper from the shop, courtesy of the scholarship

This convention has definitely fueled my love for origami even more and I can't wait to fold more :)
